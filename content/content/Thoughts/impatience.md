
A lot of times its hard to wait for the things you want to happen to happen.

But you really have to. 

Researchers are very patient for getting their idea to work. 

I think I struggle with impatience a lot, in several of my past work-related projects and in other things in life I would have been better off moving more slowly and carefully. Upon reflection, so many of the things that have worried me historically are related to the fact that things are not moving in the direction I want to be moving in yet, that the world is changing and everyone is getting better while I'm not moving towards where I want to be fast enough. Whatever part of me makes me impatient also makes me proactive and hard-working, which is what makes it a double-edged sword. However, I think it is possible to be proactive and hard-working without being impatient, by focusing a lot more on the process than the outcome.

I want to get better at this this semester.
